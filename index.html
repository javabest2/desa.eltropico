ELTROPICO

A JAKARTA-BASED F&B GROUP

ELTROPICO is a Jakarta-based F&B group that currently has 11 brands of restaurants & bars under its wing. Founded in 2013, ELTROPICO has been consistently growing and is highly committed to always bring a fresh and unique experience to its patrons. ELTROPICO's diverse concepts, which are all uniquely its own, range from Japanese, Thai, Italian, to modern Indonesian.
